OPERATORS
=========================
- Python language supports several types of operators
- Operator is symbol which applies on operands 
    ex: a + b ==> a, b operands , + : operator.
	

- Assigment operator : = 
- Arthmetic operators : + - * / ** // %
- Comparision/Relation : < <= > >= == !=
- Identity : is , is not
- Membership : in , not in
- Logical : and or not
- Bitwise: & , | , ~ , ^ , << ,>>
